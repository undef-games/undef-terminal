Interaction model

Edge bars — always visible, no names by default. Toggle "Names" button shows labels. Hover a bar to see who it is.
Tap avatar — scrolls your viewport to match that person's position. Focus ring shows who you jumped to.
Click line — pins your cursor there (📌). Others see your pin label inline on the terminal text.
Select text — highlight shows for everyone in your color. Brighter segment appears on your edge bar.
Idle fade — avatar dims after 30s. Edge bar dims after 30s. Pin labels stay (intentional markers don't fade).
